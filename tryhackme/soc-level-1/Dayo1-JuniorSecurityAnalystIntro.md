📚 WHAT I LEARNED
A Junior SOC Analyst is the first line of defence — responsible for monitoring alerts, triaging incidents, and escalating threats

The SOC operates across three tiers — Tier 1 handles alert triage, Tier 2 investigates deeper, and Tier 3 deals with advanced threats and threat hunting

Key responsibilities include monitoring SIEM dashboards, analysing network traffic, and investigating suspicious activity

SOC analysts work with a range of tools — SIEM platforms, IDS/IPS systems, and threat intelligence feeds — to detect and respond to incidents

Documentation and accurate reporting are just as important as technical skills — every action taken during an incident must be recorded
