📚 WHAT I LEARNED

Computers come in many forms — desktops, laptops, servers, embedded systems, and mobile devices — each suited to different use cases

Servers are optimised for reliability and uptime, often running 24/7 with redundant components

Embedded systems are purpose-built computers found in devices like routers, cameras, and industrial equipment

Mobile devices introduce unique security challenges due to their portability and app ecosystems

The type of computer used in an environment affects the attack surface and the security controls needed
