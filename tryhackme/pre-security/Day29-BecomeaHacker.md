📚 WHAT I LEARNED
Ethical hacking (penetration testing) involves legally and deliberately attacking systems to find vulnerabilities before malicious hackers do

Hackers are categorised by intent — white hat (ethical), black hat (malicious), and grey hat (somewhere in between)

A penetration test follows a structured methodology — reconnaissance, scanning, exploitation, and reporting

Permission is everything — hacking without written authorisation is illegal regardless of intent

Cybersecurity careers span many roles — from penetration tester and SOC analyst to malware analyst and incident responder
