📚 WHAT I LEARNED
Confidentiality — ensuring that data is only accessible to those who are authorised to see it

Integrity — ensuring that data is accurate and has not been tampered with or altered without authorisation

Availability — ensuring that systems and data are accessible and operational when needed by authorised users

Every cyberattack can be mapped to a violation of one or more of these three principles

The CIA Triad is the foundation of all security policies, risk assessments, and defensive strategies in cybersecurity
